About

I am an incoming PhD student at the University of Melbourne, co-supervised by Prof. Taehyun Rhee and Prof. Kris Ehinger. My research aims to enable AI to perceive and understand dynamic 3D environments for embodied AI. My interests include dynamic 3D reconstruction and understanding, vision-language models, multimodal large language models, and 3D scene graphs.

Previously, I received my Master's degree from Yonsei University under the supervision of Prof. Youngjung Uh, where I focused on dynamic 3D reconstruction, especially dynamic Gaussian Splatting and NeRF.
